Our Recommended Cycle Routes

Brightlingsea Colne River - 15km

This bike tour will take you through Brightlingsea so you can experience the traditional seaside town with its quaint-essential shops and streets. Then you can cycle along the Colne River to enjoy the view of the calm water and its wildlife.

Brightlingsea Wivenhoe - 24km (12km each way)

Charming tour through Brightlingsea that then guides you around the other local villages of Thorrington and Alresford before reaching Wivenhoe. Wivenhoe is situated along the Colne river, so you have the opportunity to gaze at the wildlife and even enjoy some refreshments at one of the many pubs.

East Mersea - West Mersea - 22km (11km each way)

Scenic trip through East Mersea where there are plenty of places for you stop off and have refreshments. There is an option on this route to take a detour to the Mersea Vineyard. You will also have the chance to cycle along the coastline as you make your way to West Mersea.

Point Clear - Brightlingsea - 18km

Starting off in Point Clear you can make your way around to St Osyth and through Thorrington . This route will then take you into Brightlingsea, so you can cycle passed All Saints Church and have the chance to explore the town until you reach the seaside.

Around Brightlingsea - 9km

Fantastic journey around Brightlingsea that allows you to explore our seaside town. Start off by cycling through the town centre all the way to its border. Then make your way back down to the seaside where you can take the time to stop off and enjoy the beach.
